Project Reflection:

After completing this assignment, I have a new perspective on my online identity. I used to think of social media as strictly a personal platform. When I created accounts on LinkedIn and GitHub as well as created a profile on ResearchGate, I began to think about how important it is to establish your "personal brand" in your career early on. One of the biggest difficulties I had was trying to summarize my goals in one line for my GitHub README file, which made me reflect on what I want my branch to look like in the next few years. Before I created my ResearchGate profile, I didn't think about the academic side of what I am doing in my branch. Now that I have created this profile, I see how it provides a line of communication between myself and academics in my field. I now realize that simply having these profiles is not enough. The value of these accounts will come from having continual updates to my GitHub with snippets of code, reading research papers, and networking through LinkedIn while going through my educational experience.

Draft of project work report :

This assignment includes the following to develop a body of work in relation to my digital presence, using both academic and professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn and ResearchGate. For my technical portfolio I will use my GitHub account to show my coding assignments, as well as to work collaboratively on open-source projects over the next four years. My LinkedIn account will help me to connect with alumni, follow industry leaders, document my educational achievements, and find internships. ResearchGate is a network where you can exchange research and collaborate with your peers, where I will access academic papers, keep up with current research happening in my field of study, and eventually share my undergraduate thesis or research. These three platforms together will provide me with a way to document my growth, connect with both academic and industry professionals, and create a complete digital identity prior to my graduation.
