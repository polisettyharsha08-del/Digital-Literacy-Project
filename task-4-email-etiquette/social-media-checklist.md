Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
The Do's (Responsible Use):

Do regularly review and update your privacy settings across all platforms.

Do showcase your academic projects, volunteer work, and extracurricular achievements.

Do Google your own name periodically to see what potential employers might find.

Do pause and think critically before posting, especially when you are emotional.

Do use platforms like LinkedIn and X to follow industry leaders and build a professional network.

The Don'ts (Risky Behavior):

Don't post complaining or negative comments about your professors, university, or peers.

Don't share sensitive personal information (address, class schedule, phone number) publicly.

Don't engage in heated online arguments or trolling; the internet is forever.

Don't post photos featuring illegal activities, excessive partying, or unprofessional behavior.

Don't assume that anything is truly "deleted" once it has been posted online.
